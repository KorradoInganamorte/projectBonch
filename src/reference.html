How to ask a question correctly

Check if someone has asked this question.

This way you can get the information you need faster.

Ask about the product whose card you are viewing.

If you want to know about the comparison of several products, mention them all in the wording of the question.

Do not ask questions about the delivery or service of stores.

The product can be sold in different stores with different terms of sale and delivery: questions about them prevent users from receiving information about the product.

Write briefly.

The maximum length of the question is 200 characters. You will not be able to insert links to other sites.
